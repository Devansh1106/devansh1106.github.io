# LACAM 2025 at IISER Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala, India
I attended [Latest Advances in Computational and Applied Mathematics-2025 (LACAM-25)](https://conference.iisertvm.ac.in/lacam-25/) at IISER Thirvananthapuram, Kerala, India organised by Dr. Nagaiah Chamakuri (IISER TVM, India) and Prof. Peter Bastian (IWR, Uni Heidelberg, Germany) on Dec 8-11, 2025. The theme of the conference is numerical analysis of PDEs, high performance computing (HPC), scientific computing, scalable solver for exascale architectures and developemnent of efficient and robust algorithms for numerically solving PDEs.  

Conference website: [https://conference.iisertvm.ac.in/lacam-25/](https://conference.iisertvm.ac.in/lacam-25/)
